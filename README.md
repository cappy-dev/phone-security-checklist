# 🔒 Phone Security Checklist

An interactive checklist for securing your phone. Lock down your location, block trackers, and make your device unhackable.

## Features

- **40+ actionable security steps** organized by category
- **Difficulty badges** (easy / medium / hard) so you know what you are getting into
- **Progress tracking** with a live progress bar
- **Copy-to-clipboard** summary of your progress
- **Local storage** - your progress is saved in your browser, no data leaves your device
- **Mobile-friendly** with touch-optimized interactions
- **No tracking, no cookies, no external scripts**

## Categories

1. **Everyday Essentials** - screen lock, SIM PIN, updates, 2FA, backups
2. **Hide Your Location** - app permissions, offline maps, scanning, ad IDs
3. **Network & Data Protection** - VPN, MAC randomization, DNS, Tor, public WiFi
4. **App & Permission Lockdown** - audit permissions, replace apps, clipboard snooping
5. **Advanced: Maximum Privacy** - GrapheneOS, hardware keys, burner phones, lockdown mode
